The Best Movies According to Sainath

My top 3 movies or web series of all-time.

K.G.F Chapter1

K.G.F Chapter 1 is a visually stunning and emotionally charged masterpiece that redefined the standards of Indian cinema. The movie brilliantly portrays the journey of Rocky Bhai, a young man rising from the poverty of Mumbai’s slums to the gold mines of Kolar. Yash delivers a powerhouse performance, embodying both grit and charisma with perfection. The film’s dark, gritty visuals, backed by Ravi Basrur’s thundering background score, create an intense cinematic experience. Director Prashanth Neel’s storytelling, combined with sharp editing and powerful dialogues, keeps the audience hooked from start to finish, making K.G.F Chapter 1 an unforgettable start to the saga.

K.G.F Chapter 2 takes the legacy of its predecessor to greater heights with a grander scale, stronger emotions, and breathtaking action sequences. The film seamlessly continues Rocky Bhai’s journey as he consolidates his power and faces new, formidable enemies. Yash once again dominates the screen with his magnetic presence and larger-than-life portrayal of a man driven by ambition and vengeance. The movie’s rich production design, stunning cinematography, and electrifying music amplify every moment, turning it into an epic cinematic spectacle. With its perfect blend of style, substance, and emotion, K.G.F Chapter 2 stands as a monumental conclusion to a legendary tale of power, destiny, and determination.

Squid Game

Squid Game is a gripping and thought-provoking South Korean series that masterfully blends psychological drama, social commentary, and intense survival thriller elements. The show follows a group of desperate individuals who risk their lives in a series of deadly childhood games for a chance to win a massive cash prize. With its sharp storytelling, powerful performances—especially by Lee Jung-jae—and striking visual design, Squid Game exposes the dark realities of greed, inequality, and human nature. Each episode keeps viewers on edge, balancing emotional depth with shocking twists, making it a global phenomenon that resonates far beyond its brutal games.

Julayi

Julayi is an engaging action-comedy entertainer that perfectly showcases Allu Arjun’s trademark energy, charm, and impeccable comic timing. Directed by Trivikram Srinivas, the film combines witty dialogues, smart screenplay, and a fast-paced narrative to deliver a delightful cinematic experience. Allu Arjun shines as Ravi, a clever and carefree youngster whose quick thinking and sharp mind get him entangled in an exciting cat-and-mouse chase with the antagonist, played brilliantly by Sonu Sood. With Ileana D’Cruz adding grace and glamour, Devi Sri Prasad’s foot-tapping music, and Trivikram’s punchy dialogues, Julayi stands out as a complete entertainer filled with humor, intelligence, and thrilling action—making it one of Allu Arjun’s most enjoyable performances.
